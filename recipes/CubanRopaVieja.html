Cuban Ropa Vieja

Description

Cuban Ropa Vieja is one of Cuba's most popular and beloved dishes. It is made up of shreedded beef and vegetables, and it's great served on tortillas or over rice. If you want, you can add sour cream, cheese, or fresh cilantro on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds beef flank steak
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
